Bodybuilding


Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to
control and develop one's muscles (muscle building) by muscle
hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes. It is distinct from similar
activities such as powerlifting because it focuses on physical
appearance instead of strength. An individual who engages in
this activity is referred to as a bodybuilder.

Boxing


Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing
protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand
wraps and mouthguards, throw punches at each other for a predetermined
amount of time in a boxing ring.

Fitness


Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being and, more
specifically, the ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations
and daily activities. Physical fitness is generally achieved through
proper nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical exercise, and
sufficient rest.
